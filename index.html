FAQ

Straight answers

Do you offer 24/7 emergency service?
Yes — we answer emergency calls 24 hours a day, every day, across Greater San Antonio. Average response: under 60 minutes inside Loop 410. Call (210) 555-0117 any time.
How much does a panel upgrade cost in San Antonio?
Most 200A upgrades run $1,800–$3,200 depending on home age and permits. Use the instant quote tool above for your range in 30 seconds — the final flat-rate price is confirmed on site before work begins.
¿Hablan español?
¡Claro que sí! We're a bilingual family company — the whole crew speaks Spanish and English, and this entire website is available in Spanish (ES button up top).
Do you charge a trip or diagnostic fee?
Diagnostics are $49, credited in full toward the job if you hire us. The online quote is always free.
Are you licensed and insured?
Fully: Texas state license TECL #31247, insured and bonded. A master electrician signs off on every job and we pull all city permits and inspections.
Do you offer a warranty?
Lifetime workmanship warranty on every job, in writing. Materials carry the manufacturer warranty (typically 10–25 years on panels).
